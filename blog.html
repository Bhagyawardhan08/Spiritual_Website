Vrat Katha

"Vrat Katha" refers to a collection of Hindu religious stories and legends traditionally narrated during fasting rituals, providing moral and spiritual guidance to devotees.These stories are recited during fasting to inspire spiritual reflection and adherence to religious practices.

Mandir

A mandir is a sacred Hindu temple, a place of worship and spiritual reflection. Mandirs are beautifully adorned with intricate sculptures, carvings, and vibrant colors. These temples are dedicated to various deities, each representing different aspects of the divine. Mandirs serve as a hub for religious and cultural activities, including festivals and rituals. Devotees visit mandirs to offer prayers, seek blessings.
